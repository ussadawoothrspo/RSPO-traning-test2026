Key Driver Analysis

The factor most heavily impacting the "Overall Satisfaction Score" is Moderator's time management. Effective time management significantly increases the likelihood of a higher overall score.

Performance Gap

Although the Market Dynamics topic achieved the highest score, the Smallholders topic recorded the highest standard deviation (SD). This indicates widely varied opinions amongst participants; content may require adjustments to accommodate all levels of understanding.

Voice of the Customer (Text Analysis)

Based on open-text feedback, the most frequently occurring keywords are "Workshop", "Practical", and "Supply Chain".
Recommendation: Future events should reduce lecture time and incorporate more practical sessions (Workshops/Consultation groups).
